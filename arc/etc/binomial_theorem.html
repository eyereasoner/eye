Sum of all binomial coefficients

Problem: B(n) = Σₖ₌₀ⁿ C(n,k)

Reason why (Lean 4 proof)

By the binomial theorem, (1+1)^n = ∑k=0^n C(n,k)·1^k·1^{n−k}, hence ∑ C(n,k) = 2^n. 

-- Lean 4 (mathlib) proof of  ∑ₖ C(n,k) = 2^n
import Mathlib
open Finset BigOperators

theorem sum_choose_pow_two (n : ℕ) :
  (∑ k in Finset.range (n+1), Nat.choose n k) = 2^n := by
  -- `add_pow` states: (a + b)^n = ∑ choose n k * a^k * b^(n-k)
  -- Take a = 1, b = 1 over ℕ.
  simpa using (add_pow (1 : ℕ) 1 n)
