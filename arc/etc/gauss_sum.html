Reason why (Lean 4 proof)

We justify S(n)=n(n+1)/2 using the mathlib lemma Finset.sum_range_id (Gauss’ formula for 0+1+…+(n−1)), applied to n+1. 

-- Lean 4 (mathlib) proof sketch of the formula used for the Answer
import Mathlib
open Finset BigOperators

/--
Gauss' formula, packaged for `1 + 2 + ⋯ + n`.
`Finset.sum_range_id (n+1)` states: ∑ i < n+1, i = (n+1)*n/2.
-/
theorem gauss_sum (n : ℕ) :
  (∑ i in Finset.range (n+1), i) = n * (n + 1) / 2 := by
  simpa [Nat.mul_comm] using Finset.sum_range_id (n+1)
