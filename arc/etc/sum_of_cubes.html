Reason why (Lean 4 proof)

Standard induction: base n=0 is trivial. If ∑_{k=1}^n k^3 = (n(n+1)/2)^2, then ∑_{k=1}^{n+1} k^3 = (n(n+1)/2)^2 + (n+1)^3 = ((n+1)(n+2)/2)^2 by algebra (finish with ring).
