--- Goal-Oriented Proof Explanation ---

Policies:
  (P1) Permission:  ex:alice may odrl:read ex:resourceX.
  (F1) Prohibition: ex:alice must-not odrl:use ex:resourceX.
  Both rules are unconditional (no constraints/refinements).

Action taxonomy:
  (T) odrl:read is a subclass (narrower action) of odrl:use (odrl:read ⊑ odrl:use).

Derivation:
  (S1) From (F1) and (T), Prohibition(odrl:use) ⇒ Prohibition(odrl:read).
  (S2) From (P1), Permission(odrl:read) holds unconditionally.
  (C) For any world w, Permission(odrl:read) ∧ Prohibition(odrl:read).
      Since w was arbitrary, this holds for all worlds ⇒ Global state: Conflict.
