Reason why
==========
We use the classic escape-time algorithm for the quadratic map z ↦ z² + c:
  • Start z₀ = 0; iterate zₙ₊₁ = zₙ² + c.
  • If |zₙ| > 2 at step n < max_iter, we say c escapes at n.
  • If no escape is seen within max_iter, we print the darkest shade.
Shading linearly maps the escape count n ∈ [0, max_iter] onto 10 ASCII levels.
